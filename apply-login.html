IT Intern - Job Description

Company: HINDCO Consulting Services

Location: Mumbai

Duration: 6 months Internship

Responsibilities:

  • Assist the IT team in maintaining hardware, software, and other systems.
  • Help with troubleshooting issues and provide technical support.
  • Organize and maintain IT resources.
  • Lend IT support in areas such as cybersecurity, programming, analytics, and data center management.

Requirements:

  • Currently pursuing or recently graduated with a degree in Information Technology, Computer Science, or a related field.
  • Basic knowledge of networking concepts.
  • Familiarity with various operating systems (Windows, macOS, Linux).
  • Strong problem-solving and communication skills.
  • Ability to work in a team environment.
