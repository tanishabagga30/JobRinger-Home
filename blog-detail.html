Job interviews can be a nerve-wracking experience, but with the right preparation, you can turn them into an opportunity to shine. This guide covers essential strategies to help you succeed.

Preparation is Key

Start by researching the company, understanding its culture, and reviewing the job description. Practice common interview questions like "Tell me about yourself" and prepare specific examples using the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result).
